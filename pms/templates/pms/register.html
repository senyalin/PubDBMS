Welcome to Publication Managment System (PMS)

The PMS is a platform to improve business decision by forecast training.

The PMS aims to facilitate the industry in training super forecastor to assist the managmenet layer in making better business decisions.

The PMS tries to compose a web based information management pipeline to support end to end data processing in an interactive fashion.

The PMS involves the three primary functions (a) Uncertainty calibration training, (b) Probabilistic forecast tracking and calibration, and (c) Forecast correction.
